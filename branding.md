Core Positioning
What Therai Is
Therai is a context-aware AI workspace that organizes your entire life into intelligent folders. Each folder has its own identity, memory, and AI context — like having a dedicated AI assistant for every person, project, or part of your life.

It's not just another chat app. It's your personal operating system for thinking, understanding, and growing.

The Core Insight
Most AI tools treat every conversation the same. You repeat yourself. Context gets lost. You're always starting from scratch.

Therai solves this by remembering who and what each folder represents.

A folder for yourself knows your personality, your patterns, your goals.
A folder for a project knows the vision, the constraints, the timeline.
A folder for a person knows their background, your relationship, the context.

Everything you add — chats, notes, documents, journals, images — automatically inherits that context.

Result: You think clearer. You work faster. You understand deeper.

Key Features (Complete List)
1. Smart Folders with Profiles
Every folder can have a profile — a personality, birth data, context, or identity.

For yourself: Birth chart, personality analysis, self-knowledge
For people: Understand relationships, communication styles, compatibility
For projects: Goals, constraints, timelines, stakeholders
For clients: Preferences, history, notes
For goals or habits: Tracking progress, patterns, insights
The AI adapts to each folder's identity. It's not generic responses — it's context-aware intelligence.

2. AI Cross-Examination of Everything
Therai doesn't just store your files and notes — it understands them together.

Ask questions that span:

All documents in a folder
All journal entries and chat history
Uploaded images and their context
Linked conversations
Example: "What patterns do you see across my last 10 journal entries?" or "Summarize the key insights from all the documents in this project folder."

The AI synthesizes across everything, finding connections you'd miss.

3. Shareable Folders with Fine-Grained Control
Share entire folders privately with specific people.

Choose what to share: Share the AI insights but not personal notes. Share documents but not journal entries.
Collaborative intelligence: Others see the folder's AI context and can contribute their own thoughts.
Privacy by design: You control exactly what gets shared and with whom.
Perfect for:

Sharing project folders with collaborators
Giving therapists or coaches access to your self-analysis
Working on relationship insights together
Client folders that both you and the client can access
4. Voice-First Interaction
Talk to your folders like you're thinking out loud.

Voice journaling: Speak your thoughts, Therai transcribes and understands
Voice chat: Natural conversation with context-aware responses
Background processing: Keep thinking while Therai listens
Your voice becomes another input. No typing required.

5. Multi-Modal Intelligence
Every folder understands:

Text: Notes, chats, journals, documents
Images: Upload photos, screenshots, diagrams — Therai sees and understands them
Voice: Transcribed and analyzed for emotional tone and meaning
Documents: PDFs, Word files, spreadsheets — all searchable and analyzable
Web content: Save links, articles, references
Everything works together. Ask questions across all of it.

6. Automatic Context Inheritance
When you create something in a folder, it automatically understands:

Who this folder represents
What's happened before
What matters to this person/project/goal
You never repeat yourself.
You never lose context.
You never start from scratch.

7. Self-Knowledge Engine
Therai specializes in self-analysis using:

Birth chart analysis (optional)
Personality mapping
Emotional pattern tracking
Goal setting and habit monitoring
Journal analysis over time
See yourself more clearly. Understand your patterns. Grow intentionally.

8. Private, Organized, Beautiful
Clean interface: Distraction-free, minimal, focused
Privacy-first: Your data is yours. No ads. No selling.
Lightning fast: Instant search, instant AI responses
Works offline: Core features available without internet
